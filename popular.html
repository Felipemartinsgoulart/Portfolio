What does an app need to have to become popular?

It should solve a problem for many people quickly and easily

How to solve this problem? This is the starting point when developing a successful application. First you need to detect a problem that many people go through, then think about what causes this problem and think about the solutions to this problem, finally you must think about whether you can solve this problem with technology. If you can think of all these steps and come up with the last one with a very good idea, you can start to think about the design and the coding. Good design goes beyond the beauty of the app, there is also an audience study and the experience you want to propose to your user. These rules increase the chances of success, but there isn't a secret formula. For example, there were other apps before Uber that offered the same service but only Uber was successful, so there was a bit of luck. However, you should never stop studying.
