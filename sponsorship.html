How can we make a free app without sponsorship?

Sell something to someone.

Some companies sell services directly to their users, Uber sells trips with private drivers, iFood sells food deliveries, Spotify sells access to music from around the world, all of these apps are companies that are concerned about selling a service. Some companies sell to other companies, but make the functionalities free to the customer, for example; The Weather Channel is an app that provides weather information for a particular place, this information is available to the user free of charge, but in order to access it, the user has to share their location with the app and this information is later sold to a company. Facebook also displays user information for businesses. When a user enters a shoe store fanpage and comments or likes some shoes, this information is valuable to that store.They will use it to study and how to promote the product. This is how Facebook earns part of the money. Another option is to sell items inside your app, this technique is more common in games, they can sell expansions or rare items, so you can provide the game or app for free.
