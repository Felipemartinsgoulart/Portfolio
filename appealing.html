How to make your app more appealing?

Have a good and new concept.

Pinterest wanted to inspire people like a series of magazine cutouts applied to a wall, and the way Pinterest delivers the images in a more organic format attracts people. Twitter has the same feature. Some time ago, when browsing the Internet meant going into multiple blogs and reading multiple articles, Twitter created a concept of microblogging that made people access much more content in a single refresh. All of these concepts are innovative and not at all simple, but it is these concepts that attract people. To find an innovative concept that isn't easy but possible, study and read different things, create a mental library, feed your creativity with different things, talk to many different people, travel and enjoy as much different stuff as possible. When your mind and creativity are filled with good references, it's easier to create an innovative concept.
